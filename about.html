About

Release was founded in 2017 by Zac Clark with one goal in mind: help as many people experience the gift of recovery that he was granted.

What began as a personal mission has grown into a full-spectrum recovery community built on compassion, clinical expertise, and lived experience. Our evidence-based approach supports individuals and families through every step of the healing process.
